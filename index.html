👋 Welcome!

Hi, I’m Christian G. Mendoza. If you’re here, I hope this space feels familiar, like reading the thoughts of someone who is still figuring things out and learning along the way.

I’m a second-year Computer Engineering student, but beyond that, I’m still in the process of understanding who I am becoming. I am working on building self-discipline and self-control. I am trying to form better habits and improve my focus without losing balance. More than anything, I am learning how to understand myself better.

Right now, my life feels like a process. Not a finished product. Not a clear destination. Just quiet progress happening in the background.

This website is not about showing an ideal version of myself. It exists to document the real one; the version that is still figuring things out, reflects deeply, and sometimes feels unsure but chooses to keep going anyway.

I imagine my future self reading this one day. Maybe years from now, when things feel clearer. I want him to remember what this stage felt like: the uncertainty, the small wins, and the effort it took to stay consistent.

Growth for me right now looks simple:
Better habits.
Better focus.
Better balance.
Better self-understanding.

I am trying to move at my own pace. I am keeping things steady. One step at a time. Little by little.

This space is my outlet. A place to unclutter my mind. A place to breathe. A place where thoughts that would normally stay silent finally get written down.

If you relate to still figuring things out, then maybe we are in the same season of life.

Welcome to my journey. I’m glad you stopped by.
